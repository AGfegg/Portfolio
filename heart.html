Journey to the Heart's Core

An RPG fantasy game

Role: Game designer, level designer, narrative designer, programmer
Development Duration: 6 weeks
Engine: RPG Maker MV
Platform: PC

Play Game

This game is a semi-choose you rown adventure game. Follows the main character as she shourneys across the planer in search of a magical stone to save her sister. On The way she finds the truth about a centuries old fued about her planet and its corruption

The main focus was the idea of having the heart of a hero. The determination, will power, and innocence that comes with having a good heart and hope when all seems lost. The other main focus was the idea of corruption and twisted history. When her knowledge of her planet and it's history is constanlty being challenged with each interaction as she learns the real reason why her people are suffering.

Problems and Solutions: Journey to the heart of fier

Issues in devlopment and how they were resolved

Problem

Given that it is a choose your own adventure, in the beginning of development i could see the game was branching out to far and needed a way to keep it concise.

Solution

I created root scenes that happened no matter what choices you made. Dioulgoge and certain interactions is the biggest chnage with the choices. I made it so the over arching story stayed the same and it was little side plots that branched out.

Problem

There were issues with the engne itself. Everytime i tried to do a complicated code for Ui widgets it would crash and delete my work.

Solution

I utilized the dialouge fucntion to instruct the player on what part of the game they are in, what the objective is, and the purpose. I also used actual dialouge to instruct the player. Created a very intutive map with clear pathways on where to go also helped with this issue.
