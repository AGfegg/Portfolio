Rules

King Of Conquerors

2 player game
ages 9+

In the box

  • 15 create your own character cards
  • 25 character playing cards
  • 2 dice
  • 2 royal crowns
  • 2 health trackers

Game Overview

In this fantasy card game you are in charge of your own kingdom. You will go against your opponent and protect your home or be defeated and lose it all. Will you be strong enough to defend your home from rivaling states? Will you be the king of conquerors?

How to play

Roll the dice

  • chose who is going first.
  • First player will roll the dice. The realm indicated on the dice tells you what realm you are fighting for in the current round
  • second player roll. If you get the same realm roll again until it's a different realm. 2 players CAN NOT play as the same realm
  • the plater that rolls a free choice can choose which realm to fight with even if its already been selected
  • If realm taken from you by a free roll, roll again and get a different realm

Pick Your cards

  • Each player chooses 2 character cards out of their designated realm’s deck. These will be the cards you will fight with in the current round.

The fight!

  • second player will start
  • State your attack out loud and how much damage it does. Your opponent must subtract that number from their health. You cannot use the same attack 2 times in a row. You can use the same character in a row.
  • After your opponent has subtracted your attack from their health it is their turn to state their attack against you. If both of your character cards are out of health you lose and the player with any surviving character cards wins the round.
  • The winner can reuse their realm or roll a new one. The loser must roll for a new realm. They cannot use the realm they lost with. If the winner decides to roll again, their realm is available to be used.
  • First player to get to 2 wins in a row receives the royal crown. The player holding the royal crown can choose at random an additional playing card from their realm’s deck.

Who Won

  • There are 5 rounds. Whoever wins the most rounds is the king of conquerors.

Now that you've read the rules, go into battle to see who is the King of Conquerors.
