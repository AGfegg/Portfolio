Zachariah's House

A suspenseful puzzle game

Role: Game Designer, Level Designer,Narrative designer, 3d Modeler, Programmer
Development duration: 12 weeks
Team Size: 3
Engine : UE5
Platfrom: PC

Play Game

A first person Escape room game. Exploring the idea that obsession and being stuck in th past can grow into a serious problem. The player is trapped in his uncles basement. The uncle, Zachariah, has become a hermit and a bit of a hoarder. The player will explore the home and see what happens whrn untreated trauma and nostalgia take form in an obsessive hoarding nature.
