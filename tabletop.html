Table Top Games

A series of Card and board games

King of Conquerors

A trading card game

Role: Game designer, Artist
Rule book

In this fantasy card game you are in charge of your own kingdom. You will go against your opponent and protect your home or be defeated and lose it all. Will you be strong enough to defend your home from rivaling states? Will you be the king of conquerors?

King of conquerors is targeted towards 9-12 year olds in order to boost creativity as well as master social and strategy skills. The game's backstory is that there are five realms that once lived in unity and are now fighting to defend their land from each other. Those realms being, the soldiers of the night, the wind keepers, the soldiers of the sun, the blacksmiths, the lost kingdom.

Time Kills

Mystery card game

Role: Team Lead, Game designer,Artist
Rule book

Famous celebrities are being killed in their prime throughout time. The person behind it stole a time machine and is using it to cause mayhem in the past and present. You and your team of interdimensional detectives have to figure out who and how they are killing celebrities based on clues and connections between the murders. The goal is to solve the case before they get through all 5 eras/decades.
